Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the MathSwipe: Brain Challenge app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by htoznaye (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as an Ad Supported service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (IP address)
  • Device Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID / IDFA for ad personalization)
  • Operating system version and device performance characteristics
  • In-app gameplay data, high scores, and statistics

The Application does not gather precise GPS location data.

The Application does not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to process personal data.

Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services to aid the Service Provider in improving the Application and displaying relevant advertisements.

The Application utilizes third-party advertising, analytics, and infrastructure services that have their own Privacy Policies regarding data handling. Below are the links to their Privacy Policies:

  • Google Play Services

Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling it. You may use the standard uninstall processes available on your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace.

Data Retention & Deletion Policy

The Service Provider will retain User Provided data for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter. If you would like your personal data or account information deleted, please visit our Data Deletion Page or contact us directly at htoznayegames@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

The Service Provider does not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13.

If the Service Provider discovers that a child under 13 has provided personal information, it will be deleted immediately. If you are a parent or guardian and become aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Security

The Service Provider is committed to safeguarding the confidentiality of your information and provides physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect it.
