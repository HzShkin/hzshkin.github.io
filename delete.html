Data Deletion Request

To request the deletion of your account and all associated data in the MathSwipe: Brain Challenge app, please follow these steps:

  • Send an email to: htoznayegames@gmail.com
  • Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request - MathSwipe"
  • In the body of the email, please specify your User ID or the email address used for Google Login.

We will process your request and delete all your personal data from our records within 7 business days.

Please note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your data is deleted, your progress, scores, and account information will be lost forever.
