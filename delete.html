How to request account deletion

If you wish to delete your account, game progress, and all personal data associated with MathSwipe: Brain Challenge, please follow the steps below:

1. Send an email to:
htoznayegames@gmail.com
2. Subject line:
Data Deletion Request - MathSwipe
3. Body of the email:
Specify your User ID or the email address linked to your Google Play Games account.
⚠️ Warning: This process is permanent. Upon request, all your high scores, unlocked themes, coins, and leaderboard ranks will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
